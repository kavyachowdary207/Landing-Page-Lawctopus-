Six-month roadmap

From contract fundamentals to commercial drafting and freelancing.

Step 01 / Month 1

Essential clauses

Contract skeletons, recitals, definitions, operative clauses, boilerplate terms, and networking basics.

Execution and negotiation

Stamp duty, registration, witnesses, signatures, contract labs, and negotiation table exercises.
